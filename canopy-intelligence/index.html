Canopy Intelligence

Canopy Intelligence turns open satellite imagery and ecological models into per-tree and per-parcel decisions — no proprietary data required. Below are the three places clients usually start. Find the one that sounds like you.

Cities & Urban Forest Managers

Know your canopy, meet wildfire code, plant for equity

Stand up a tree inventory from satellite — no ground survey needed — then put it to work: flag which trees a new wildfire-resiliency code would force out when a building is renovated, map canopy & heat equity to find neighborhoods that need shade most, and prioritize replacement and planting. A single, defensible picture of trees as green infrastructure.

Live demos: Steamboat tree & drought-risk map · Steamboat wildfire compliance map

Land Trusts & Conservation Easements

Catch unauthorized cutting from free satellite imagery

Did someone harvest where the deed says they can't? I detect small, sub-clearcut forest disturbances from free satellite imagery and adjudicate each one against the parcel's permitted activity — defensible, reproducible, and built to run without paid data. Useful for easement stewardship and improved-forest-management carbon verification.

Retrospective case study available on a call → book a time

Arborists & Urban Forest Managers

Score every street tree for the climate it will live in

Every street tree scored for 2050 climate and pest risk, with diversity-aware planting recommendations and the dollar value at stake — so replacement and planting budgets go where they matter. Stand-level forest forecasting (composition, biomass, management scenarios) is available for woodland and natural-area planning.

Live demo: Philadelphia tree map — all ~142,000 street trees
